ABOUT US

Welcome to Corner Bean Café, your cozy spot for exceptional coffee and community connection. Founded in 2024 by Pavithra Nakshatri, we’re passionate about delivering not just great coffee, but a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our beans are carefully selected and expertly brewed to bring you the perfect cup every time. Our café is more than a place to grab a drink—it’s a gathering spot where you can relax, meet friends, and enjoy local events. We’re proud to be a part of this community and look forward to sharing our love of coffee with you. Come visit us and experience the Corner Bean Café's difference! Warmly, The Corner Bean Café Team
